Treatment :

To treat downy mildew in pumpkin leaves, apply fungicides such as copper-based sprays or chlorothalonil early to prevent the disease from spreading. Improving air circulation by spacing plants properly, removing weeds, and avoiding overhead watering helps reduce moisture buildup, which favors fungal growth. Additionally, planting downy mildew-resistant pumpkin varieties provides long-term protection against the disease.
